About Lantic

A Tradition Since 1888

Lantic is a leading refiner, processor, distributor and marketer of sugar products in Western Canada. It's sugar products include granulated, icing, cube, yellow and brown sugars, liquid sugars and specialty sugars and syrups.

Lantic Inc. came into existence after the merger of Lantic Sugar Limited and Rogers Sugar Ltd. in June 2008 which resulted in the successful establishment of Lantic Inc. today.
